Software Developer

Full-Time

BSc in Computer Science, 5+ years of experience in Web Development.

Job Description

We are seeking a skilled software developer to join our team. The ideal candidate should be proficient in web development technologies and have a strong understanding of full-stack development.


Responsibilities include collaborating with teams to design and develop software solutions, debugging and troubleshooting, and staying up-to-date with industry trends.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field.
  • At least 5 years of experience in software development.
  • Proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and backend technologies like Node.js or Python.
  • Experience with databases such as MySQL or MongoDB.

Work Experience

Previous work experience should include building and deploying web applications, with a focus on high-performance solutions.


Ideal candidates will have worked in fast-paced environments, and demonstrated the ability to work effectively both independently and as part of a team.


Experience with version control systems such as Git and familiarity with Agile methodologies are highly preferred.
